Swimming Pool

Serving as the focal point of Cuore Hotel is our picturesque swimming pool, sitting at the center of our grounds. Bask beneath the balmy skies in one of our lounge beds, get a drink from Pool Bar, or pre-order a meal from the poolside. Our open floorplan allows spaces to flow seamlessly from one area to the next, so you're close to everything Cuore has to offer.








Buffets

Bringing fresh ingredients and flavors to your everyday dining experience, we serves a fusion of comfort food and polished recipes. Sourcing ingredients from the best local suppliers on the island, Cuore offers delicious dishes at affordable prices.








Restaurants and Cafe

Our vibrant retail complex gathers a unique crop of homegrown restaurants, bars, and commercial establishments under one roof. Station X is a melting pot of various cuisines, exciting new concepts, and curated boutiques that bring out the rich personality and uniqueness flavors.








Bar Lounge

There is a full range of bar drinks including a wide selection of spirits, Whisky, Brandy and Liqueurs, soft drinks and beer including and Falkland Islands only real ale. The wine list includes a variety and styles of wine including the famous Montes premium wine produced in Chile, with wines by the bottle and glass.








Wellness and Spa

Our Rasul treatment has been updated to create a skin-smoothing self-treatment that blends mineral-rich mud, gentle steam and heat to provide an experience that cleanses and rejuvenates. Mud is self-applied to the body, hair and face. The dry heat dries mud and allows the skin to draw out impurities from the body.
